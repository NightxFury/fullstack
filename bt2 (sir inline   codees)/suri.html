Shuri

Shuri is the brilliant and inventive princess of Wakanda, known for her unmatched intelligence and technological genius. As the sister of King T'Challa (Black Panther), she plays a crucial role in advancing Wakanda's technology, creating state-of-the-art gadgets, suits, and weapons. Beyond her technical prowess, Shuri is courageous, witty , and compassionate, often using her skills to protect her people and innovate for the greater good. She represents the perfect blend of leadership, intellect, and heart.
